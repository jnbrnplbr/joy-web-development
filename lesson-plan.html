(June 10, 17 & 24) 		1. Define Mechanical Drawing and CAD 1. Determine the importance of CAD. 1. Create simple house plan through manual drawing. progeCAD
Drawing Hard p. 3-5
A. Engaging Activity

   The teacher will let the students play “pass-the-message”. (Mechanical drawing meaning)

Example: Mechanical Drawing are architectural or engineering plans created with hard tools. It is sometimes called “blue prints”

B. Discussion

   The teacher will discuss the difference of Manual Drawing and CAD. The students are expected to recite using their books.

Responsibility in demonstrating proper way of using computers.
4-6 (July 1, 8 & August 15) 1. Demonstrate understanding in opening CAD software. Starting with CAD Software p. 6-8
A. Engaging Activity

   The teacher will let the students observe the CAD Interface. After which, the teacher will let the students label the parts without looking at their books.

B. Discussion

   The teacher will check and discuss the CAD Interface. The teacher will use the tarpapel CAD Interface that was answered by the students.

C. Wrap up/ Generalization
Guided Question:

“Demonstrate the steps on how to open CAD and describe the parts.”
